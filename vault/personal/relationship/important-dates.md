# Important Dates

> OpenClaw checks this daily and creates prep tasks automatically.
> Format: YYYY-MM-DD for one-time, MM-DD for recurring annual.

## Recurring Annual
- Partner birthday: MM-DD (auto-create prep task at -14 days)
- Your birthday: MM-DD
- Anniversary: MM-DD
- Parents' birthdays: MM-DD

## One-Time Upcoming
- Example: Visa renewal — YYYY-MM-DD (start process at -30 days)
- Example: Lease renewal — YYYY-MM-DD

> Add dates as they come up. OpenClaw handles the reminders.
